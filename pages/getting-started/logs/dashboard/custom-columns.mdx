# Custom logs columns

Adding custom columns to the logs page is super easy!

<p style={{ display: "block ruby"}}>Head over to the `Logs` page and click on the <span><CogIcon /></span> icon on the right-hand side.</p>

<div style={{ marginTop: 8 }}>
<img src="https://r2.fivemanage.com/logs_custom_columns_cog.png" />
</div>

Here you can add custom columns to the logs page. Just search for the column you want to add and click/enter to add it.

<div style={{ marginTop: 8 }}>
<img src="https://r2.fivemanage.com/logs_custom_columns_settings_menu.png" />
</div>

## Customzing columns

Once you've added a custom column, you will see it on the table.

<div style={{ marginTop: 8 }}>
<img src="https://r2.fivemanage.com/logs_custom_colums_table.png" />
</div>

### Column width

Clicking on the column name will open a menu where you can change the width of the column, or remove it.

<p style={{ display: "block ruby"}}>You can also remove a custom column by clicking on the <span><CogIcon /></span> icon and then clicking on the `x` on the custom column name</p>

<div style={{ marginTop: 8 }}>
<img src="https://r2.fivemanage.com/logs_custom_columns_table_menu.png" />
</div>
